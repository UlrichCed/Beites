About us

A multisectoral expertise firm dedicated to sustainable development

The Bureau of Expertise and Engineering in Territory, Environment and Health - International (BEITES-I SARL) is a professional expertise firm providing training, studies, advisory support, project and programme evaluations, and the supply of biomedical equipment.

Founded in March 2019, our firm draws on the individual experience of its members in international consulting and disaster management, each with more than 10 years of experience.

Learn more about BEITES-I
  • A team of committed, professional consultants
  • Strong experience at associative, private and public levels
  • Multisectoral action: Health, Environment, Education, Prevention
  • Ambition to become a national and international benchmark firm
