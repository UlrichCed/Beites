About us

An expertise firm committed to health, the environment and sustainable development.

Our story

Founded in March 2019, serving sustainable development

The Bureau of Expertise and Engineering in Territory, Environment and Health - International (BEITES-I SARL) is a professional expertise firm providing Training, Studies, Advisory support, project and programme evaluations, and the supply of biomedical equipment, set up to fulfil a commitment to sustainable development.

BEITES-I operates in a multisectoral framework focused on Health, Environment, Education, Prevention and Disaster Management, whose synergy is the threshold of effective sustainable development. BEITES-I is made up of a core team of committed, professional consultants for its various missions, with strong experience at associative, private and public levels.

Founded in March 2019, our firm draws on the individual experience of its members in international consulting and disaster management, each with more than 10 years of experience.

Our mission

  • Respond actively and professionally to any request for technical support, studies or capacity building.
  • Contribute to achieving sustainable development and community goals.
  • Apply scientific research methods to research or evaluation questions.
  • Collaborate closely with stakeholders to maximize the relevance of our work.
  • Share our expertise through university teaching and scientific publications.

Our vision

To be a benchmark study firm at national and international level in public health, sustainable development, education, disaster management and humanitarian issues.

Our ambition is to be a driving force for expertise and leadership in sustainable development.

Our principles

To carry out our missions and achieve our vision

  • Act fully in the service of humanitarian action
  • Act for the environmental cause in all its dimensions
  • Deliver quality work based on professional rigor
Our values

What defines us every day

  • Respect for commitments
  • A sense of innovation
  • Diverse fields of expertise
  • Transparency
  • An inclusive partnership policy
  • A culture of integrated approach
Our objectives

Strategic and operational support

Through its vision and commitment, BEITES International intends to contribute to the development and proposal of development strategies, and above all to support private and public organizations in executing their strategies, through training, studies, and monitoring & evaluation of projects and programmes, in Burkina Faso and internationally.

More specifically, BEITES International's purpose is production/design, transformation, marketing and capacity building.
